FAQ


What are the risks?
We guarantee our investors the confidentiality of personal data and the safety of funds. However, remember that any investment is always associated with risk, which is proportional to the return. Therefore, we have selected the optimal ratio of profitability and risk when drawing up an investment plan.
How to start?
To become a member of the system, register. Registration is free and will take a couple of minutes. Enter your personal account by entering your username and password, select a payment system, tariff plan and open a deposit. After opening a deposit, you will automatically become a member of the system and will receive a profit from your investment.
How to register on the company's website?
To register, click "Register" and fill in all the required fields.
How do I access my account?
To access your account, you must enter your personal account, specifying the login and password received during registration.
Can I be sure that my data is safe?
Yes, the company guarantees our investors the protection and security of personal data. We do not transfer personal data of clients to third parties! Our site is protected from various types of attacks, and all transmitted data is encrypted.
Who can become a member of the system?
Anyone who has reached the age of majority can become a member of the system.
How many accounts can I create?
You can create only one account in the system. If this condition is violated, the site administration will take action.
How to open a deposit?
In order to open a deposit, register, enter your personal account and replenish your account.
How many deposits can I open?
There are no restrictions on the number of deposits created.
Can I change the details in my account?
Yes. To do this, enter your personal account and select the appropriate section. Save the new data. If you cannot change anything, please contact our support team.
I can't log into my personal account?
In such a case, check if you entered the data correctly. If the data is entered correctly, clear the cache and try again. If the problem persists, write to support.
What are the benefits of participating in an affiliate program?
An affiliate program is a great opportunity for every investor who wants to get additional income. However, you do not need to have an active deposit.
Do you have an affiliate program?
Yes, we have prepared a generous affiliate program for you: 5%, 2%. Please note that you will receive profit from a partner only if he went to our site using your referral link, registered and created a deposit through replenishment of the account.
Do I need a deposit to participate in the affiliate program?
No, you don't need to have an active deposit. All you need to do is to distribute your referral link by any means available to you.
How to become a member of the affiliate program?
In your personal account, you will find your referral link. Distribute it by all means available to you and invite people. New users will go to our site using your link and register. After that, they will automatically be assigned to you. You will receive an affiliate reward from all account deposits they make.
Where can I find an affiliate link?
You can find your affiliate link in your personal account.
Why do I need an affiliate link?
An affiliate link is needed so that with its help you invite new investors to the system, and it, in turn, automatically determines that they came from you.
What do you need to know about the accrual of affiliate commission?
Affiliate rewards will be received every time your affiliate deposits an account. Enrollment is received instantly. However, it should be remembered that the partner reward will be credited to the wallet of the payment system in which your partner opened a deposit.
How to track the results of acquisition?
You can view information about your affiliate income in your Personal Account.
How can I check my balance?
You can check your balance at any time by logging into your personal account.
How soon will my deposit be credited?
Usually, funds are credited instantly. However, it should be noted that the time of crediting may depend on the regulations of the payment systems themselves.
Is there a commission for replenishment and withdrawal of funds?
No, there is no commission in the system. The investor pays only the commission of the chosen payment system, if any.
I still have questions, where can I contact?
If you did not find the answer to your question in the list of frequently asked questions, write to the support service.
